About

About Me

I am passionate about music and have been playing the piano for 10 years. This site is an opportunity for me to discuss a topic that moves and inspires me. Through this platform, I wanted to explore not only the richness of the piano but also its role in our modern society. My goal is to provide a space where everyone can rediscover the piano and, more generally, the emotional power of music.

The Topic

In a future where emotions are regulated by neurological implants and music has been relegated to the past, our site invites you to explore what we have lost—and what we might regain. Each article aims to be a window into a time when music played a vital role in our emotions and reinforced our humanity. Ultimately, I chose this topic because I love new technologies and observing their advancements, but I am also interested in the past. I believe that to know where we are going, we must understand where we come from and not forget the first steps of evolution in a creative process. For me, the idea of rediscovery is essential to our development.

"Rediscover pure emotion through the keys of the future"
